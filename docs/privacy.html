Privacy Policy

Snore Notes is an offline-first application. All data you enter is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage (Application Support directory) and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal data.

The only network communication in this app is Apple's own StoreKit for processing in-app purchases and subscriptions, which is handled entirely by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it. We have no servers and no access to your data.
